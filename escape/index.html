Medical Escape Room

You find yourself trapped in a mysterious medical facility. Can you solve the puzzles and escape before it's too late?

The Scenario

A deadly new virus is sweeping the globe. You, a brilliant virologist, have been kidnapped and brought to this secret lab. You must find the cure and escape before time runs out.

Your Goal

Your mission is to solve a series of puzzles to identify the correct antiviral drug and the container it's stored in. The fate of the world rests in your hands.
